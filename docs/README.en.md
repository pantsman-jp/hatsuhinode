# First Sunrise 2025-2026

## Overview
This is a tool that simulates sunrise.
Sunrise begins after the number of seconds specified by the user.

Enjoy the atmosphere of the first sunrise of the year right in your browser, without having to wake up early and brave the cold.

## How to Use
1. Open the page
2. Enter “Seconds until sunrise after pressing start”
3. Press the start button, and the sun will begin to rise after the specified number of seconds
