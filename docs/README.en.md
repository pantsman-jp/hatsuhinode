# First Sunrise 2025-2026

[日本語](https://github.com/pantsman-jp/first-sunrise/blob/main/README.md)

<https://pantsman-jp.github.io/first-sunrise/>

## Overview
This tool simulates a sunrise.
The sunrise begins after the specified number of seconds.

Enjoy the atmosphere of the first sunrise of the year in your browser
without having to wake up early or brave the cold.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_sunrise)

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html`.
2. Enter “Seconds until sunrise after pressing start”.
3. Press the Start button. The sun will then begin to rise after the specified number of seconds.
